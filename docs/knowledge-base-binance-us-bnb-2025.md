### Order Types

#### Market Order
- Executes immediately at current price
- **Use**: When you need to enter/exit fast (scalping)
- **Fee**: Taker fee (0.1% or 0.075% with BNB)

#### Limit Order
- Executes only at your specified price
- **Use**: When you want specific entry price
- **Fee**: Maker fee (0.1% or 0.075% with BNB)

#### Stop-Limit Order
- Triggers limit order when price hits stop price
- **Use**: Stop losses, take profits
- **ScalpStorm**: Use for automated stop losses
