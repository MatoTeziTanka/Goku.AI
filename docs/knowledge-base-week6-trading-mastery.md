# PART 2: ADVANCED TECHNICAL ANALYSIS

## üìà Advanced Chart Patterns

### Elliott Wave Theory
**Concept**: Markets move in predictable 5-wave impulse + 3-wave correction

**Impulse Waves (Bullish)**:
- Wave 1: Initial rally
- Wave 2: Correction (never below start)
- Wave 3: Strongest rally (1.618x Wave 1)
- Wave 4: Smaller correction
- Wave 5: Final rally (exhaustion)

**Correction Waves**:
- Wave A: Down
- Wave B: Bounce (fake rally)
- Wave C: Final drop (capitulation)

**Trading Strategy**:
- Buy end of Wave 2 or 4
- Sell end of Wave 5
- Short start of Wave A or C

### Wyckoff Method
**Concept**: Smart money accumulation/distribution cycles

**Accumulation Phase**:
1. Preliminary Support (PS): First bounce
2. Selling Climax (SC): Panic selling
3. Automatic Rally (AR): Dead cat bounce
4. Secondary Test (ST): Retest lows
5. Spring: Fake breakdown (trap sellers)
6. **BUY HERE**: Markup begins

**Distribution Phase** (opposite):
1. Preliminary Supply (PS)
2. Buying Climax (BC)
3. Automatic Reaction (AR)
4. Upthrust (UT): Fake breakout
5. **SELL HERE**: Markdown begins

### Fibonacci Retracements
**Key Levels**: 23.6%, 38.2%, 50%, 61.8%, 78.6%

**How to Use**:
1. Find swing high and low
2. Draw Fibonacci from low to high (uptrend)
3. Price retraces to Fibonacci levels
4. **Buy at 61.8% or 78.6%** (strongest support)

**Example**:
- BTC rallies from \$30k to \$40k
- Retraces to \$36,180 (61.8% level)
- **Buy signal** ‚Üí next leg up to \$50k+

### Harmonic Patterns

**Gartley Pattern** (Bullish):
- XA: Initial move
- AB: 61.8% retracement of XA
- BC: 38.2-88.6% of AB
- CD: 127.2-161.8% of BC
- **Buy at D** (reversal point)

**Bat Pattern** (more accurate):
- Similar to Gartley
- B = 38.2-50% of XA
- **Higher win rate (70%+)**

### Volume Profile Analysis
**Concept**: Price levels with most trading activity

**Volume Point of Control (VPOC)**:
- Price level with highest volume
- Acts as strong support/resistance

**How to Use**:
1. Enable Volume Profile on TradingView
2. Identify high-volume nodes (HVN)
3. Low-volume nodes (LVN) = fast price moves
4. Trade bounces off HVN

### Advanced Indicators

**1. Ichimoku Cloud**
- **Above cloud**: Bullish (buy)
- **Below cloud**: Bearish (sell)
- **Inside cloud**: Consolidation (wait)
- Tenkan/Kijun cross = buy/sell signal

**2. On-Balance Volume (OBV)**
- **OBV rising, price flat**: Accumulation (bullish divergence)
- **OBV falling, price flat**: Distribution (bearish divergence)

**3. Stochastic RSI**
- More sensitive than RSI
- <20: Oversold (buy)
- >80: Overbought (sell)

**4. VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price)**
- Institutional reference price
- **Above VWAP**: Bullish
- **Below VWAP**: Bearish

### Multi-Timeframe Analysis (Critical!)

**Top-Down Approach**:
1. **Monthly**: Long-term trend
2. **Weekly**: Medium-term trend
3. **Daily**: Short-term trend
4. **4H**: Trade setup
5. **1H**: Entry timing
6. **15min**: Precise entry

**Example**:
- Monthly: Bull market (uptrend)
- Weekly: Consolidation
- Daily: Bullish flag forming
- 4H: Breakout imminent
- 1H: Wait for retest
- **15min: Enter long at support**
