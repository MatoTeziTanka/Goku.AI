## âš¡ LEVERAGE EXPLAINED (5x-100x)

### What is Leverage?
Borrow money from exchange to amplify your position

**Example**:
- **No leverage (1x)**: $80 buys $80 worth of BTC
- **5x leverage**: $80 controls $400 worth of BTC
- **10x leverage**: $80 controls $800 worth of BTC
- **100x leverage**: $80 controls $8,000 worth of BTC

### Leverage Risk
**1% price move**:
- 1x: $0.80 gain/loss
- 5x: $4 gain/loss
- 10x: $8 gain/loss
- 100x: $80 gain/loss (ENTIRE ACCOUNT!)

### **Liquidation** (Most Dangerous Part)
If market moves against you, exchange closes position automatically

**Liquidation Examples** (100x leverage):
- BTC at $40,000, you long (buy) with 100x
- BTC drops 1% to $39,600
- **You're liquidated** = entire $80 GONE

### Recommended Leverage for ScalpStorm

#### Phase 1: Month 1-2 (Learning)
**Use 3x-5x leverage ONLY**
- Liquidation buffer: ~20-33% market move
- Safe enough to learn
- Still amplifies gains

#### Phase 2: Month 3-6 (Intermediate)
**5x-10x leverage** (if profitable in Phase 1)
- More aggressive, more potential

## ğŸ¯ SCALPSTORM TRADING STRATEGY

### What is Scalping?
- Very short-term trades (1-30 minutes)
- Small profit targets (0.5%-2% per trade)
- High win rate needed (60%+)
- Many trades per day (5-20)

### ScalpStorm Setup

#### Timeframes
- **Primary**: 5-minute chart (trade execution)
- **Confirm**: 15-minute chart (trend confirmation)
- **Bias**: 1-hour chart (overall trend)

#### Indicators (Keep It Simple)
1. **EMA 9 & 21** (trend direction)
2. **RSI 14** (overbought/oversold)
3. **MACD** (momentum confirmation)
4. **Volume** (confirmation of moves)

### Entry Rules (ALL must be true)

#### Long Entry (Buy)
1. Price above EMA 21 (uptrend on 15m chart)
2. EMA 9 crosses above EMA 21 on 5m chart
3. RSI between 30-70 (not overbought)
4. MACD histogram turning green
5. Volume increasing

#### Short Entry (Sell)
1. Price below EMA 21 (downtrend on 15m chart)
2. EMA 9 crosses below EMA 21 on 5m chart
3. RSI between 30-70 (not oversold)
4. MACD histogram turning red
5. Volume increasing

### Exit Rules

#### Take Profit
- **Target 1**: 0.5% (take 50% position off, move stop to breakeven)
- **Target 2**: 1% (take remaining 50% off)

#### Stop Loss
- **Always use stop loss**: 0.5% below entry (long) or above entry (short)
- **Never move stop loss against you** (discipline!)

### Risk:Reward Ratio
- **Minimum 2:1** (risk $1 to make $2)
- ScalpStorm typical: **1:1.5 to 1:2**
- Win rate target: **60%+**
